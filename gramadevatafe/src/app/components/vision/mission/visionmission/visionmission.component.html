Our Village - Our Temple - Our Deity

Gramadevata Foundation is a pioneering organization dedicated to the revival, preservation, and promotion of Sanatana Dharma and Hindu cultural heritage. Rooted in the ancient Indian principle of "Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam" (The World is One Family), the foundation's core philosophy revolves around creating a unified global Hindu Community that is deeply connected to its spiritual roots. The foundation’s efforts span across multiple domains, including temple preservation, community engagement, cultural education, and rural upliftment, all designed to foster a strong, vibrant, interconnected and an Empowered Hindu society.

Vision

The vision of Gramadevata Foundation is to "Connect, Communicate, and Coordinate" with the spiritual and cultural roots of Sanatana Dharma. The foundation aims to cultivate a sense of family and unity among Hindus worldwide by bridging the gap between modern life and ancient traditions. By leveraging technology and community-driven initiatives, the foundation envisions a world where every Hindu remains connected to their heritage, actively participates in spiritual and cultural activities, and contributes to the global Hindu community.

Mission

  • Gramadevata Foundation’s mission is multifaceted, focusing on empowering Hindutva through various platforms that cater to different aspects of Hindu life.

  • Protecting and revitalizing sacred temples across India, ensuring they remain active centers of spiritual practice and community engagement

  • Encouraging the practice of traditional rituals and customs in villages and urban areas alike, helping to sustain these practices for future generations.

  • Building a global network of Sanatana Dharma organizations to collaborate on cultural, trade, and political initiatives, thus amplifying the impact of Hindu values on the world stage.

  • Providing access to knowledge and resources on Hinduism, including scriptures, rituals, cultural arts, and traditional crafts, to educate and empower individuals.

  • Supporting rural artisans and small-scale producers by providing them with platforms to sell their products, thereby preserving traditional crafts and improving livelihoods.
